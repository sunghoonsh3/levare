Our Mission

Levare is dedicated to breaking down barriers to education for those who need it most. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality learning, regardless of their circumstances or background.

Who We Serve

Single Parents

Flexible learning opportunities for those balancing work and childcare responsibilities.

Those Facing Housing Insecurity

Learning pathways designed to lead to stable employment and financial security.

Low-Income Workers

Upskilling opportunities to help transition into higher-paying careers.

Education Gap Fillers

Alternative pathways for young adults who were unable to complete formal education.
